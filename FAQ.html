F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Shoe size

Shoe size varies by manufacturer. When shopping for shoes, make sure they are the right size and fit your feet. The toe box should be rounded or oval to match the shape of your feet. There should be about a fingertip of space between your longest toe and the tip of the shoe.

Shoe fit

When standing, make sure there is about 3/8" or 1/2" between your longest toe and the end of the shoe. Walk around in the shoes to make sure they are comfortable and don't rub or chafe. Your heel should not slip or slide while walking

Shoe type

when choosing shoes for long walks, avoid heels and opt for breathable shoes with a rounded sole and small cuts for flexibility. The tread should be asphalt-resistant.

Shoe purpose

When shopping for shoes, consider what activities you will be doing in them
